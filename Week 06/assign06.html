Mission Statement

Maintaing the vision of our founder, Ivanro Lagazo, Back Alley Institute of Technology (B.A.I.T) gives all applicants the opportunity to succeed. By developing attributes of success within each student, students will leave B.A.I.T with a quality education and the necessary skills needed to change the world.
